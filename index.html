PROJECTS

PokeChess Unite - 2021

PokeChess is a game that we made for our CCPROG3 class. The programming language that we used was Java, so we had to follow the rules of OOP using the Model-View-Controller. This project I would say was the hardest even among even tho this was something that we did a year ago. OOP for me was very hard to grasp, and I think that goes for most people. I was in charge of the battle mechanics of the game.

General Gameplay

Like the name suggests, its Pokemon combined with chess. Depending on the type of Pokemon, you can move 1-3 spaces adjacently or diagonally. Here, you can battle an opposition pokemon if it is within range, and however wins gets the oppositions pokeballs. To score, you must go to the oppositions side and then dunk the pokeballs you have, just like the Pokemon MOBA.
